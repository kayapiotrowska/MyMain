Hi! My name is Kaja Piotrowska and I am an automation engineer/ programmer with 5 years of experience. During that time I have been responsible for entire software development life-cycle, including PLC/HMI programming, developing the standard, system tests, commissioning and project management. I have spent most of the last 3 years abroad, working in several departments of my company gaining experience among international teams.

Recently I have developed passion for websites development. I spend most of my evenings as wess as weekends expanding my knowledge by reading, excercising and preparing websites for my friends. I have started with analyzing templates to start with and to get to know good practises, and when I have analyzed a few of them, I started preparing websites on my own! I work to make my work responsive, modern, to use the good practises and to make them animated and interesting for the user.
